The Problem

Our live event/activity app was designed to achieve travel experience options for people regardless of their financial situations. We have observed that there is no one tool to discover organize budget events and activities during trips, which is causing this dissatisfaction of booking any travel experience. How might we improve travel organization and diversity so that our customers are successful based on making sure their needs and ones are met?

Solution

Our solution is Grapevine. Grapevine is a live activity/event app that helps any traveler carry a unique travel experience based on the traveling limitations such as, budget budget consciousness, group collaboration and time. Working with his limitations ensures the user is still able to have the full travel experience they desire.

Key Learning from User Testing

Our research mainly consisted of interview others about their travel expereinces to get a better understanding of someones needs and wants when traveling. We found that there is a wide range of needs and wants for people who want to travel and that creating something that could be a flexible travel app for many different types of users would help make our app unique.
Grapevine made finding new events and activates easy to find for travelers. Being able to travel and do something that the average person might not know about makes for a unique travel experience that anyone can be apart of.

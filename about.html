ABOUT KAYLIN

Kaylin is a Tampa, born artist who works primairly in sculpture using found furniture, fabrics, and other mixed media materials to create exaggerated furniture forms. She creates situations in which these everyday objects are altered or detatched from their naturak function. By applying specific combinations and certian manipulations, different functions and context are created to these objects. She also finds it important to seduce her viewers by emphasizing aesthetics in most of her work.

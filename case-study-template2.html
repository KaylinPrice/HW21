The Problem

The Stray Cat Alliance's website is outdated and not user friendly.

Solution

We believe that it is critical to create a more user friendly site to help increase adoption numbers for the city of Los Angeles. By creating a site that is easy for anyone to navigate it will create an easier process for someone who is looking to adopt a cat

Moodboard Inspiration

Our inspiration for this redesign of Stray Cat Alliance’s website was to really bring in strong bold colors that make this site come to life. Adopting a cat or any animal is an exciting and joyous time and we wanted to really capture that emotion into our redesign.
View Prototype

Key Learning from User Testing

During our next round of user testing we saw a significant improvement with our overall results, primarily based on the increase in UI Design to the prototype. But we did get additional feedback that did help us with our hi-fi prototype.   Based on this feedback we made improvements to the buttons and overall layout making it more consistent across the whole page. 
We found that the Stray Cat Alliance website didn't visually represent such an amazing non-profit organization very well. Adding a compelling color pallet and making the overall user interface fun / lively helped bring Stray Cat Alliance back to life.
